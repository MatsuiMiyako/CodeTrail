Lesson 1: Printing in Python

🧠 What You'll Learn

  • How to use the print() function
  • Printing strings, numbers, and combining them

📘 Explanation

The print() function is used to display information on the screen. You can print text, numbers, or even results of math.

print("Hello, world!") print(5 + 3) print("Your score is", 95)

🎯 Practice Time

Try printing the following:

  • Your name
  • Your favorite number
  • A combination of both
# Your turn! print("My name is ____") print(42) print("My favorite number is", 42)

💡 Bonus Tip

Use double quotes " " or single quotes ' ' to define strings. They both work!

❓ Quiz

What will this print?

print("2 + 2 =", 2 + 2)

Answer: 2 + 2 = 4
